Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bike rental service

Renting a bike is simple! Download our app, create an account, find a bike near you using the map, scan the QR code to unlock, and you're ready to ride. Return the bike to any designated station when you're done.

We offer flexible pricing: Pay-as-you-go at $1 to unlock + $0.15 per minute, or choose our value packs like the Day Pass for $15 (unlimited 30-minute rides for 24 hours). Monthly subscriptions start at $25/month.

Yes! We provide free standard helmets at all our stations. You can also rent our premium Smart Helmets with advanced safety features for just $5 per ride.

Our Smart Helmets connect to your phone via Bluetooth. They feature built-in GPS navigation with turn-by-turn directions through bone conduction audio, an SOS button that alerts emergency contacts if you fall, and an AI voice assistant to help with directions and ride information.

For immediate assistance, press the SOS button on our Smart Helmet or call our 24/7 support line at 1-800-ECO-RIDE. For non-emergencies, use the Help section in our app to report issues or request a replacement bike.
